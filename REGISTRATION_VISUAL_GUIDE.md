**Scenario**: Sarah wants to create a workspace for her startup

1. **Lands on /register**
   - Sees OBSOLIO logo and "Create your account"
   - Step 1 indicator shows 4 steps total

2. **Selects "Organization"**
   - Clicks organization card
   - Checkmark appears, Continue button enables
   - Clicks Continue

3. **Sees Organization plans**
   - Org Free, Pro, Team, Enterprise shown
   - Toggles to "Yearly" to see 20% savings
   - Sees "Org Pro: $99/mo (save $240/year)"
   - Clicks "Org Pro" card
   - Clicks Continue

4. **Fills account details**
   - Enters: Sarah Johnson, sarah@startup.com
   - Selects: United States, enters phone
   - Creates password, sees "Strong" indicator
   - Confirms password
   - Clicks Continue

5. **Sets up workspace**
   - Types "my-startup" for workspace URL
   - Clicks "Check" â†’ Sees "âœ“ Available"
   - Enters "My Startup Inc" as org name

6. **Email verification sent**
   - Sees success message
   - Email sent to sarah@startup.com
   - Redirected to verification pending page
